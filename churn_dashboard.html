Recommendations derived from XGBoost feature importance, churn pattern analysis, and segment-level revenue modeling across 100,000 customers.
🔴 Priority 1 · Fiber optic · #1 Feature (26.3%)
Investigate and fix Fiber optic service quality
Fiber optic customers churn at 57.1% — by far the highest segment. With 44,216 Fiber customers in your base, this single segment accounts for the majority of churn. Root cause: audit support ticket volume, outage frequency, pricing vs DSL, and NPS scores by service type. Immediate action: proactive SLA guarantee + auto-credit for any outage longer than 4 hours. Introduce a "Fiber Loyalty Lock" — 2-year price guarantee + free speed upgrade — for all M-t-M Fiber customers below 12 months tenure.
Expected churn impact −5% churn rate · ~$1.1M/yr saved
🔴 Priority 2 · Contract type · #2 Feature (22.8%)
Convert month-to-month customers to annual plans
Month-to-month customers churn at 47.7% vs 8.0% on two-year plans — a 6× gap. 55,000 customers (55% of base) are on M-t-M. Target the highest-risk cohort: M-t-M + Fiber optic + tenure 3–9 months. Offer a 15% discount for switching to a 1-year plan, or 20% for a 2-year plan. A 12% conversion rate on the M-t-M base secures ~6,600 additional customers and reduces the overall churn rate by ~4 percentage points.
Expected churn impact −4% churn rate · $905K/yr saved
🟠 Priority 3 · New customers · Critical 0–6 month window (53.9%)
Structured 90-day onboarding & early loyalty program
53.9% of customers in months 0–6 churn. This is the single highest-risk tenure window. Implement: (1) Welcome call + setup audit at day 7, (2) Usage health check at month 3 with personalized tips, (3) $25 loyalty credit at month 6 milestone, (4) Annual plan upgrade offer at month 9. Customers who survive past 24 months churn at only 18.1% — surviving the first year is the key inflection point. Estimated 8,500+ new customers enter this window annually.
Expected churn impact −3% churn rate · $680K/yr saved
🟠 Priority 4 · Electronic check · 44.8% churn
Auto-pay migration campaign for electronic check users
Electronic check customers churn at 44.8% — 2× the rate of auto-pay customers (21.8–23.6%). At 100K scale, this pattern is statistically robust. Electronic check is a proven proxy for low engagement and high payment friction. Action: Permanent $5/month discount for switching to bank transfer or credit card auto-pay. Run automated outreach at months 2, 4, and 6. Secondary benefit: reduces failed payment support tickets and late-payment churn.
Expected churn impact −2% churn rate · $453K/yr saved
🔵 Priority 5 · Add-on services · Switching cost lever
Bundle security and support add-ons for new and at-risk customers
NumAddons is a top-5 feature. Customers with more add-ons (Online Security, Tech Support, Online Backup) churn significantly less — each service increases switching cost and perceived value. Strategy: 60-day free trial of Online Security + Tech Support for all new signups. For high-risk customers with zero add-ons: 40% discount for the first 12 months. Target the 18,000+ high-risk customers currently using zero add-ons with a personalized offer via email and in-app notification.
Expected churn impact −1.5% churn rate · +$22 ARPU per customer
🟢 Priority 6 · Loyalty milestones · Long-term retention
Loyalty milestone rewards at 12, 24, and 36 month anniversaries
Customers who reach 48 months churn at only 9.9% — tenure is protective. Loyalty rewards at key anniversaries create positive anchoring and reduce the incentive to evaluate competitors. Program: $20 bill credit or free speed upgrade at 12 months, free add-on for 3 months at 24 months, permanent 5% loyalty discount at 36 months. This also generates positive word-of-mouth. Pair with a referral bonus program to further extend LTV.
Expected churn impact −1% churn rate · LTV +18% for milestone customers
